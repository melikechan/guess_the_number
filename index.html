How to play?

Make a guess between 1-100. Send your guess and the game will tell you if it is high or low.
When you make the correct guess, you will earn 3-11 points randomly.
If you could not make the correct one, we will drop 2 points per wrong guesses.
Once you make the correct guess or you spend all the guesses, you can restart again.
